# Excel-Sales-Analytics
Sales & Finance Analytics Project
Sales Report
Project Objectives
Developed a Customer Performance Report to analyze customer-wise sales performance and identify high-value customers.
Conducted a comprehensive comparison between Market Performance and Sales Targets to evaluate target achievement across different markets.
Purpose of Sales Analytics

Sales analytics enables businesses to monitor, measure, and evaluate sales activities and overall performance, leading to informed decision-making.

Importance of Analyzing Sales Data
Identifies sales trends and patterns.
Tracks key performance indicators (KPIs).
Highlights opportunities for growth and improvement.
Supports data-driven business strategies.
Role of Sales Reports
Helps determine effective customer discount strategies.
Facilitates negotiations with customers using performance insights.
Identifies potential expansion opportunities in high-performing markets and countries.
Finance Report
Project Objectives
Created Profit and Loss (P&L) Reports by Fiscal Year to evaluate annual financial performance.
Developed Monthly P&L Reports to monitor short-term profitability trends.
Generated Market-wise P&L Reports to assess financial performance across different regions and markets.
Purpose of Financial Analytics

Financial analytics supports the evaluation of organizational performance, enhances decision-making, and improves communication with stakeholders.

Importance of Analyzing Financial Data
Enables benchmarking against previous periods and industry standards.
Provides a foundation for budgeting and forecasting.
Helps identify areas requiring financial improvement.
Role of Financial Reports
Aligns financial planning with organizational strategic goals.
Builds confidence in the company's financial outlook.
Supports management in making informed business decisions.
